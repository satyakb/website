Impulse
Impulse is an online marketplace that capitalizes on the impulsive tendencies of today's consumers.

The premise of the app is that buyers get a fixed time limit (~8 seconds) to decide whether or not they want to buy the product. After the time limit is up, the user never sees the product again, and loses, potentially, a great opportunity. Impulse is also similar to eBay in that users can upload their own products onto the website for others to buy.

This app was built during PennApps Spring 2014 by Reed Rosenbluth, Keshav Varma, and myself.
